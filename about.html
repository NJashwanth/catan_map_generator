About

The story behind the Catan Map Generator.

What This Site Is

Catan Map Generator is a free, in-browser tool for creating random, balanced Catan-style boards. It runs entirely in your browser — nothing to install, no account, no data sent anywhere. Pick a player count, optionally enter a seed, and get a board your group can rebuild with their physical tiles in under a minute.

Why It Exists

Shuffling terrain hexes by hand tends to produce lopsided islands: three ore tiles in one corner, a 6 next to an 8, and one player starts the game already ahead. This generator applies simple fairness rules — spacing out the high-probability number tokens and spreading resources — so setup is fast and the starting positions are worth debating, not predetermined.

Seeds and Sharing

Every board comes from a seed. Share the seed (or the full URL, like /?seed=gamenight&players=6) and anyone can regenerate the exact same board on their own device — handy for remote setup or settling arguments about what the board looked like.
