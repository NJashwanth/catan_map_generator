Overview

Catan Map Generator (catan-boardgame-map-generator.web.app) is a free browser tool. Board generation happens entirely on your device: the seeds you type, the boards you create, and your control settings are never uploaded, stored, or shared by us. This site does not require an account and does not ask for personal information.
